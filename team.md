# Ben Nelson 

Ben's main area of area interest is in the philosophy of law, and specifically on the unpublished aspects of positive law.  In addition, he is working on projects in the pragmatics of intellectual good faith, the epistemology of intuitions, and evolutionary game theory in the philosophy of the social sciences.  A unifying feature of his work is the idea of tacit normativity, which is an idea he finds weird, and therefore fascinating.
